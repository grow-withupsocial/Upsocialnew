Payment Configuration Guide

• Korapay: Get your keys from the Korapay dashboard. Use test keys for development.

• Webhook: Copy the webhook URL above and add it to your Korapay dashboard settings.

• Bank Transfer: Enable for users who prefer offline payment. You must verify manually.

• Auto-approve: Deposits under this amount will be credited instantly without review.

• KYC: Users making deposits above this limit must complete identity verification.

• Changes take effect immediately for new transactions. Existing pending payments are not affected.
